About Us

The Maadi Model United Nations (MUN) Team is a dynamic and ambitious student organization dedicated to simulating the work of the United Nations. Based in the heart of Maadi, Cairo, our team brings together passionate students from diverse backgrounds who share a strong interest in global affairs, diplomacy, public speaking, and problem-solving.

At its core, the Maadi MUN Team aims to educate and empower youth by providing them with a platform to explore complex international issues in a structured and intellectually stimulating environment. Through regular training sessions, mock debates, and participation in local and international MUN conferences, our members develop essential skills such as critical thinking, negotiation, leadership, and effective communication.

Vision & Mission

At Maadi MUN, we aim to inspire and empower high school students through engaging simulations of the United Nations. By fostering a deep understanding of international relations, diplomacy, and public speaking, we provide our members with the skills and confidence to make a real impact in their communities and beyond.

Our vision is to build a generation of globally aware youth who are equipped to tackle complex global challenges with empathy, strategy, and leadership. Through hands-on sessions, debates, and real-world simulations, students step into the shoes of diplomats, address world issues, and represent nations with integrity and knowledge.

We strive to prepare students to become confident, informed, and articulate leaders who can collaborate to build a better future — this is the heart of Maadi MUN.

Our mission extends beyond the academic realm. We foster a strong community where members develop teamwork, resilience, critical thinking, and cultural understanding. Maadi MUN is more than just a club — it's a movement of young changemakers shaping tomorrow.
