Conference History

2022

Climate Action Conference

On the 10th of February 2024, the Maadi MUN team gathered to discuss global climate challenges. Delegates presented powerful ideas for sustainable energy, conservation efforts, and global cooperation. Students wrote articles, proposed action plans, and collaborated with local organizations to spread awareness.

The conference also included a green campaign in local neighborhoods and schools, encouraging youth to plant trees and reduce plastic use. It was an inspiring event that empowered young leaders to act on climate change.
