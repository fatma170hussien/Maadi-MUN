MUN Community Outing

MUN Community

Let's reflect on the remarkable achievements of our young diplomats during this season's outing. It was a wonderful chance for them to break the ice, strengthen their bonds, and expand their social connections. Alongside engaging in activities like the jumping rope contest, card games, and a spirited football match—each of which offered a blend of joy and meaningful interaction with peers—the event served as an ideal gateway to introduce the theme of mental well-being in a subtle yet impactful way.

This memorable experience highlighted the incredible talents within our community. We extend our deepest appreciation to all those who showcased their abilities, enriching the outing with energy, creativity, and a shared sense of purpose—bringing our theme of mental well-being to life throughout the season.
